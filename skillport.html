Pop-up Blockers

Step 1: Find and click "More button".

Within Chrome, locate More Button (three vertical dots) in the top right corner.

Find and click on the Settings.

Step 2: Scroll down and click "Advanced".

Click on Advanced.

Step 3: Find and click "Site Settings".

Within the Privacy and Settings section, click Site Settings.

Step 4: Find and click "Pop-ups and redirects"

Within Permissions, click on Pop-ups and redirects.

Step 5: Click "Add" to add site.

Within Pop-ups and redirects, find Allow section and click Add to add another site.

Step 6: Type [*.]skillport.com into new field box. Then press Add

After [*.]skillport.com has been added, follow the same steps to enter [*.]skillwsa.com and [*.]skillsoftcompliance.com.
