Pop-up Blockers

Within Chrome, locate More Button (three vertical dots) in the top right corner.

After clicking More Button (three vertival dots) you will see a drop down menu. Within the drop down menu, find and click on the Settings tab.

In the Settings page scroll all the way down until you see Advanced. Click on Advanced and you will see a Privacy and Security section.

Within the Privacy and Settings section, locate and click Site Settings.

Next looking within Permissions to find and click on Pop-ups and redirects.
