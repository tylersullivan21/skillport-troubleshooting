Clearing the Browser Cache

Step 1: Find and click "More button".

Within Chrome, locate More Button (three vertical dots) in the top right corner.

Find and click on the Settings.

Step 2: Scroll down and click "Advanced".

Click on Advanced.

Step 3: Find and click "Clear browsing data".

Within the Privacy and Security section, click Clear browsing data.

Step 4: Select "cached images and files" then click clear data

While Cached images and files is selected click Clear data
